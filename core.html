CORE Exercises

The Core area (Belly) of your body is located between your Chest and your Pelvic area. Major muscles around this area are: Rectus Abdominis (outer Abs), Transverse Abdominis (Inner Abs), Obliques, and Lower Back (Erector Spinae). Our core muscles are major stabilizers in every functional movement we make that's why strengthening it is very important. A weak core will show a poor posture and Human Gait (limb movement)

Sprawl to push ups hand

Target: Abdominals, Pectorals(chest), Shoulders(anterior) , Triceps

Type: Compound

Priority: Supplementary

Difficulty: Hard

Abdominal Situps

Target: Abdominals

Type: Isolation

Priority: Supplementart

Difficulty: Easy

Plank arm taps to push ups

Target: Abdominals, Pectorals(chest), Shoulders(anterior) , Triceps

Type: Compound

Priority: Supplementary

Difficulty: Hard
